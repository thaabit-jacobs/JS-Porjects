Saladin

Statue of Saladin with a plaque stating "Best soldier on Earth"

Saladin or Al-Nasir Salah al-Din Yusuf ibn Ayyub was a Sunni muslim born 1137 and died 4 March 1193. Saladin was the first to be the sultan of both Egypt and Syria and become the founder of the Ayyubid dynasty. Saladin is well know for his miltary campaigns that he led againgst the Crusader states in Levant.
